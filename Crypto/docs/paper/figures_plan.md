# Figures Plan

## Figure 1. System Overview

Show:
- Research
- Engineer
- Evaluate
- Decision
- Publish
- strategy registry
- artifacts and logs

Caption idea:
- End-to-end architecture of the governance-aware AI-silo for strategy validation.

## Figure 2. Candidate Funnel

Show counts:
- proposals generated
- candidates engineered
- candidates evaluated
- candidates passed
- strategies approved

Caption idea:
- The platform behaves as a selective validation funnel rather than a permissive ranking engine.

## Figure 3. Rejection Reason Distribution

Use:
- `paper_results/rejection_reasons.csv`

Chart:
- horizontal bar chart

Caption idea:
- Distribution of candidate rejection causes in the current audit snapshot.

## Figure 4. New vs Mutation Comparison

Use:
- `paper_results/source_type_stats.csv`

Chart:
- grouped bars for pass rate and mean Sharpe

Caption idea:
- Comparison between newly generated candidates and mutation-derived candidates.

## Figure 5. Cross-Asset Stability

Use:
- `paper_results/candidate_metrics.csv`

Chart:
- scatter plot of `sharpe_mean` vs `sharpe_std`

Caption idea:
- Candidates with strong average Sharpe may still be rejected for high cross-asset instability.

## Figure 6. Regime Stability

Use:
- `paper_results/candidate_metrics.csv`

Chart:
- scatter plot of `sharpe_mean` vs `sharpe_regime_std`

Caption idea:
- Regime variance provides a complementary notion of robustness beyond average performance.

## Figure 7. Mutation Lineage

Use:
- `paper_results/lineage_edges.csv`

Chart:
- parent-child lineage graph

Caption idea:
- Lineage-aware registry and mutation-based strategy evolution.
